Curtis Hardy

Front-end Developer

Hi, I'm Curtis Hardy a 20 year old passionate about the web and technology, based in Bournemouth UK. I build modern responsive websites that are aesthetically pleasing and have rock-solid code backing up a pixel-perfect appearance.

As a curious autodidact I'm a fast learner who has a desire to be the best I possible can be. I find communicating with, and being a part of, a team an incredibly rewarding experience as I believe this brings out the best possible ideas and innovations. But also thrive in an individual environment. My keen eye for detail, organisation and eagerness to deliver a quality result at the end of a project are all part of my focused approach towards work.

My main skillsets are in the front-end, with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery and CSS frameworks making up the majority of those skillsets, I also have basic knowledge of PHP and mySQL. Experience in CMS, SEO, creative software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects and Sony Vegas, and IT support are also skills I possess.

Outside of my professional life I am an avid gamer and sim racer, taking part in a handful of eSports and leagues. I love to listen to lots of music and have a taste that stretches across the whole musical spectrum, with Michael Jackson being my all time favourite. I'm a huge Formula One fan, and of motorsport in general, rarely missing a race. I also enjoying films and TV shows, with my favourite films being the Star Wars saga.
